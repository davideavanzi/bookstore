About

Bookstore is a small and simple online store, developed by three PoliMi students, that offers some unusual books for purchase.

How We Started

We met at the Hypermedia Applications course at Politecnico di Milano and decided to work together on this project, determined to get a good product to deliver at the exam and learn the latest technologies in web design.

Along with this web application, we provide some documents analyzing our developement process and the design choices we took while building this website.

Free Shipping

Shipping will be free for all purchases, in order to promote our website. Enjoy!

Return policy

We offer a return policy for books bought within 14 days: just write us in the contact section and we'll give you all the instructions.

Reader value

We believe in the value of the reviews of any reader, so for every book you'll be able to write a review.
